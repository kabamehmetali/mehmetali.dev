Simple Calculator Chrome Extension

This is a basic calculator Chrome extension designed for quick and easy mathematical calculations directly within your browser. With a clean, user-friendly interface, it provides essential arithmetic operations to help you compute on the fly.

Features

The extension offers addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. It includes functions to clear all and delete the last digit, supports percentage and negative sign toggling, and delivers responsive button interactions along with full keyboard support.

Installation

Download or clone the repository. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/, enable "Developer mode", click "Load unpacked", and select the extension directory. The calculator icon will then appear in your toolbar.

Usage

Click the calculator icon in your toolbar to open the extension, and use the on-screen buttons or your keyboard to perform calculations.

Files

The project includes key files such as popup.html (the main UI), scripts.js (calculator logic), styles.css (styling), manifest.json (configuration), background.js (setup), and privacy-policy.html (privacy policy).

Privacy Policy & License
